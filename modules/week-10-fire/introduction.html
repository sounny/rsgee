Active Fire Monitoring

This lab will teach you how to access, process, and explore three fire monitoring datasets in the data catalog. Fire monitoring worldwide benefits from raw satellite imagery and processed fire-mapping datasets. Google Earth Engine supports fire monitoring throughout fire seasons with satellite data from sources like Landsat 8, Sentinel-2, and MODIS. It also hosts multiple fire datasets from GOES and FIRMS.
