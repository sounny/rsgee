This assignment is worth 25 extra credit points to your Lab total grade. The field of remote sensing is constantly evolving, with new technologies and techniques emerging rapidly. In this extra credit assignment, you will have the opportunity to explore and analyze the future directions of remote sensing and, in the process, contribute to shaping the future of this exciting field.

Instructions:

  1. Find a research paper, article, or other literature published within the last five years (2018-2023) that presents a groundbreaking concept, innovative technology, or novel approach in remote sensing. This may involve satellite systems (e.g., Landsat, SRTM, Sentinel), private remote sensing (e.g., Planet, Maxar), aerial photography, drones, AI, image classification, or other related topics.

  2. Write a 2-3 page paper that includes the following: a. A summary of the selected literature and an explanation of its significance to the future of remote sensing. b. A critical analysis of the new developments presented in the literature, including the potential benefits, limitations, and challenges. c. Your insights and reflections on how these advancements might shape the future of remote sensing, considering potential applications and their impact on society, the environment, and scientific discovery. d. A discussion of your vision for the future of remote sensing, inspired by the selected literature and your understanding of the field. What innovations would you like to see, and how might they be achieved?

  3. In addition to your paper, submit a digital copy of the selected literature for reference.
