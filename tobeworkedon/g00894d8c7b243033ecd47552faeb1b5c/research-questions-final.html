Here you will submit your final research questions.  Take into account the feedback you got on the draft and narrow down your question.  You want the scope of your work to allow for you to finish your work this semester.  Also, you want to get your question to something that can be answered.  Submit a document with:

  1. Clear research question(s)
  2. A hypothesis to the research question(s)
  3.  Narrative giving some background about the question, rationale for using remote sensing to study it, and context for the study area selected.
