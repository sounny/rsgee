Throughout the semester, you will work to do novel research using Remote Sensing and Digital Image Processing.  To start this series of work, I would like you to brainstorm some research questions on your interests.  This will be the draft of the research questions section of your final term paper.  For the scope to fit this class, you may have to have a very simple research question.  The requirements of your research question are:

  • You utilize Remote Sensing Image Processing Platforms like ENVI, ERDAS, Google Earth Engine, or ArcGIS Pro.
  • You Process Remote Sensing Data Products to create new derivative datasets
  • You create a visualization to communicate your results and enhance your analysis. 

For the research question, it is your choice if you want to do a Remote Sensing Application or if you want to do pure Remote Sensing research. 

Examples of Pure Remote Sensing Research:

  • Error Propagation through different scales of remote sensing products
  • Pixel mixing or remote sensing data.
  • Evaluations of various products for set workflows
  • Evaluate various approaches to the same problem and dataset to see impacts on conclusions. 
  • Development of novel processes for remote sensing datasets

Examples of Applied Remote Sensing Research:

  • Measuring dynamics of landscape changes due to climate change. 
  • Studying edge effects of conservation zones of ecosystem
  • Determining deforestation rates in regions
  • Estimating carb sequestration from forest regrowth
  • Hydrological modeling with elevation models to determine the Hydrography of an area

The list can go on forever.  What you want to do is submit a 1-page summary of what you would like to study to get some feedback and guidance on how to formulate the final question and scope of your project.  

NOTE: If you are a graduate student, I would like you to formulate your question on the research topic you are doing for your thesis or dissertation.
