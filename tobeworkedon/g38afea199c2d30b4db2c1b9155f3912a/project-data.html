Now that you have an idea of your research questions, you need to start looking at the datasets that may possibly limit your work.  Most remote sensing research is the art of advancing science with very limited data.  Many times our research questions are limited but data availability.  I want you to look out for what datasets you could use for your project.  Sometimes with imagination and innovation, you can figure out ways to answer your questions with limited data.  Outline what data you will use, where you will get it, and what type of processing you plan on doing with it.
